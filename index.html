Frost Spell

After you defeat the ice golem a blue symbol appears on the ground.Realizing that is something like an ice spell,you quickly write it down on a piece of paper,before it fades away.

Spell has no effect on monsters in ice region. PvP: after you win the fight, you can use the spell to lock the player for 2 turns. Fire region: +4 power to any monster, including the fire dragon.

Frozen Greed

After defeat the noble adventurers were bound by Seregost in a magic ice so they can be forever obedient and remember the mistake of going against an elder dragon

The ice covering the armor is comming directly from the dragon's breath. Charged with magic, will not melt even in front of the hottest flame, thus canceling the effects of the fire land.

Ring of Ecthelion

Ecthelion was once a frost mage, using the same magic like Seregost.Though not having the same power as the dragon, in his tower of ice, gleaming like a spike of pearl and silver, he did create some fine items.

You can only lose this ring by going back from the start, multiple uses. +2 power for every fight with a fire minion, except Balrog the Ashbringer.

Frost Fang

At the beginning, dragons weren't all bad. They had a lot of goodness inside until they decided to put it away. And so they performed a magic ritual where they poured everything that was good in them in to one item, that they guarded with their life. For Seregost, it was Frost Fang,the doom of fire.

+2 power against PvP +3 power against any fire minion +5 power against Balrog the Ashbringer

Fishing Spear

You chose to spend some time on the island to look around for something that could help you in your travels. And soon enough you do find a spear. Used mostly for fishing it could come in handy in any fight, after all is pointy and sharp, what could go wrong there.

+2 Power in PvP. +2 Power against any monster.

Poseidon Trident

Poseidon was thought to be the god of seas and with the Trident in hand, he could control the weather, tame the storms, or destroy those who would oppose him with the power of water.To hold such a mighty weapon in your hand, almost makes you feel unstoppable.

+2 Power in PvP. +3 Power against any monster. +4 Power against fire land monsters.

Tattered Sail

From the wreckage of those less fortunate treasure hunters you manage to save a tattered sail. Though it has seen better days it seems to be good enough for you to gain some speed in crossing the lake.You take this as a sign of good luck and continue your journey with the wind at your back.

For every roll in Lake of Atlantis you get +2 movement speed.

Water Spell

Udoras was once one of the greatest elf mage and in his greed for more power he destroyed the entire city he once swore to protect.Tricked and then cursed by the dragons, now he try to atone for his sins by helping you defeat them.

Whenever you lose a fight that force you to return from the start of the game, you can use this Revive Spell to have one more chance at defeating your opponent ( single use ).

Phoenix Gift

"As long as there is life, a flame will always burn". No matter how dangerous fire is , we will always need it in our lives, so in a way, fire is immortal. This is the power that you now control, the endurance of the Phoenix.

Use this spell after you were defeated to come back and have another chance.

Rock Splitter

At first a giant rock golem was guarding the entrance in to the fire land, up until a group of brave heroes defeated it. They took out the golems lava heart and brought it to a blacksmith who then use it to create this axe with a blade forever warm and sharp enough to cut even through the hardest stone.

+2 power in PvP. +4 power against monsters.

Will of the Commander

Never surrender, never retreat ! This is the law that all commanders follow and no one is above it. Now that you brought peace to the general you can feel his rage,like a bull on fire, unstoppable, relentless.They say you need an army to defeat a dragon, but now, you are the army.

Single Use: If you were defeated or locked in place for 3 turns or more, use the comander will to revive or break free.

Nature Wrath

A drop of rain can turn in to a storm, a gentle gust of wind in to a hurricane, roots as thin as a thread of hair, can grow bigger and stronger than the giant anaconda, able to devour even the tallest skyscraper. Never underestimate the power and wrath of nature.

+2 Power against ice and water monsters. +4 Power against fire monsters.
